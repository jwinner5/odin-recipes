Oyakodon

Description

A mix of chicken and egg simmered in a soy based sauce served over rice

  • 1/4Lb chicken
  • 1/2 sliced onion
  • 2 egg
  • 1 cup rice
  • 1 Tbsp sake
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp mirin
  • 1/2 cup dashi
  1. 1) Gather and prep ingredients
  2. 2) Mix sake, soy sauce, mirin, and dashi
  3. 3) Simmer onion to sweat
  4. 4) Add chicken and 2/3 of the sauce
  5. 5) Once fully cooked add in whisked egg
  6. 6) Once the egg has started to set remove from heat and let risidual heat finish cooking the egg so it doesnt become firm
  7. 7) Place on top of bowl of rice and serve
