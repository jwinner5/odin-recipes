Shokupan

Shokupan

Description

A Japanese style of bread made with milk that is springy and soft

  • 250g warm water
  • 20g sugar
  • 7g salt
  • 10g honey
  • 7g yeast
  • 350g bread flour
  • 20g milk powder
  • 25g butter
  1. 1)Gather all ingredients
  2. 2)Combine warm water, sugar, salt, honey, and yeast, let sit for 10 minutes to bubble
  3. 3)Combine bread flour and milk powder, mix well
  4. 4)Add butter and kneed until it becomes a glossy
  5. 5)Rise for an hour
  6. 6)Punch down, roll out, and fold into thirds
  7. 7)Let rise for 3 hours
  8. 8)Place into shokupan tray and finish proofing
  9. 9)Preheat oven to 425
  10. 10)Bake for 25-30 minutes
