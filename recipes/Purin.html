Purin

Description

A soft and silky custard commonly called purin or pudding

  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 2 Tbsp Sugar
  • 2 Tbsp boiling water
  • 4 sheets of gelatin
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 80g sugar
  • 1 3/4 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  1. 1) Gather all ingredients and 8 4-oz ramekins
  2. 2) Combine sugar (2Tbsp) and boiling water (2 Tbsp) in a pan, cook on medium till golden brown
  3. 3) Immediately remove from heat, place on damp towl to stop cooking and add 2 Tbsp cool water to thin
  4. 4) Pour into ramekins evenly
  5. 5) Cut up gelatin sheets and soak in 1/4 cup water
  6. 6) Melt gelatin in double boiler
  7. 7) Mix egg yolks and sugar, wisk till pale and smooth
  8. 8) Heat milk over stove till warm
  9. 9) Slowly temper eggs with warm milk, then add back to sauce pan
  10. 10) Cook over low heat, stirring constantly till custard coats the back of a spoon
  11. 11) Add gelatin and whisk, strain the mixed liquid to remove any clumps
  12. 12) Add remaning milk, cream, and vanilla, whist till homogenous
  13. 13) Divide into ramekins and chill in fridge overnight
